Graph Visualization Style

The Graph page is the centerpiece. How should nodes and relationships be rendered?

A: Force-Directed Graph

Nodes float and self-organize by connections. Color-coded by type (Resolution=purple, Error=red, Pattern=green, Module=blue, File=yellow). Drag to explore, click to inspect. Classic knowledge graph look.

B: Hierarchical / Layered

Organized in layers: Team → Modules → Resolutions → Errors/Fixes. Shows the ownership hierarchy clearly. Good for understanding structure, less organic.
